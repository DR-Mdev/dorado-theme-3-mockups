Method

One lane. One question.
One verdict.

A lane is not a segment name. It is a testable question, like: will mid-size financial firms buy multilingual moderation as an external API?

LANE

The question

One core question about the market. Hypotheses inside it can drift, morph or fork. The question stays accountable.

PROBE

The batch

A verification batch: who, angle, channel, planned volume. Every probe closes with a verdict.

LOG

The record

One contact, one record. Logs attach through probes, never through a static 3,000-row list. The hypothesis is a lens, not a box.

Every probe ends in one of these. Hover them.

MODE 01

Discovery

20-60 touches per cycle · settled in weeks

Fail fast and cheap. Ranges stay wide by design, because narrow promises are how time-selling sneaks back in.

MODE 02

Cultivation

Multi-touch · deep research · about a month

When a segment bites, we go deep: ICP, multiple touches, real research per account. Progress is measured in reach and relationships, not counts.

Physics One cultivation lane at a time, per operator. That constraint is what keeps every plan honest.
