Privacy Policy.

How Dorado LLC collects, uses, and protects personal information across this website and our services, written to be read, not skimmed past.

1. Who we are

Dorado LLC ("Dorado", "we", "us") is a Japan market-entry consulting firm providing research, validation, and advisory services for international companies exploring the Japanese market. Our registered office is at Hamamatsu-cho Dia Building 2F, 2-2-15 Hamamatsu-cho, Minato-ku, Tokyo 105-0013, Japan. For anything in this policy, contact us at support@dorado-group.jp.

2. What we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Your name and company affiliation
  • Email address and other contact details you choose to share
  • The contents of messages you send us through contact forms, email, WhatsApp, or LINE

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser and device specifications
  • Pages visited and navigation patterns on this site
  • Referral source (how you arrived here)

Automatic collection happens through cookies and similar technologies managed via Google Tag Manager. See our Cookie Policy for details and choices.

3. How we use your information

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Evaluating whether our services fit your situation, and scoping a potential engagement
  • Follow-up communications you would reasonably expect
  • Managing contacts and enquiries via our CRM
  • Site analytics and improvement
  • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance with legal obligations

4. Sharing and vendors

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We use a small number of service providers who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements, including: Google LLC (analytics and tag management), HubSpot (CRM), Automattic (website hosting), Meta (WhatsApp communications), LY Corporation (LINE), LinkedIn, X Corp., and YouTube (social and embedded content).

5. International transfers

Some of the vendors above operate outside Japan, primarily in the United States and South Korea. Where personal information is transferred internationally, we do so in accordance with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI). Information about the data protection frameworks of destination countries is available on request.

6. Retention

DataHow long we keep it
Inquiry submissionsDuration of our relationship, plus 3 years
CRM recordsDuration of the business relationship, plus a reasonable period
Email & messaging historyDuration of the relationship, plus a reasonable period
Analytics dataPer Google Analytics defaults (26 months at the user level)

When data is no longer needed for the purposes above, we delete it or anonymize it.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable legal obligations, you may request:

  • Confirmation of whether we hold personal information about you, and access to it
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Suspension of use of your information

Send requests to support@dorado-group.jp. We will verify your identity before processing a request.

8. Contact and complaints

Questions and complaints about this policy can be directed to support@dorado-group.jp. You may also lodge a complaint with Japan's Personal Information Protection Commission (ppc.go.jp).

9. Changes to this policy

We update this policy periodically. Material changes are reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Appendix · Japan: additional APPI disclosures

For the purposes of the APPI: the business operator handling personal information is Dorado LLC (address above). Retained personal data is used for the purposes listed in Section 3. Procedures for disclosure, correction, and suspension requests, details of third-party provision to overseas parties, and complaint mechanisms are as described in Sections 5, 7, and 8 of this policy.
