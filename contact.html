Before you write

Three things people
usually want to know first.

Do we need to commit to a retainer to start?

No. Advisory plans start at $99/month, cancel any month; execution starts at a single $250 scope. Nothing scales up until the earlier stage has actually proven something.

Will this turn into a sales pitch?

If the honest answer is "this isn't a fit" or "you're not ready for this yet," that's what you'll hear. We'd rather say that once than run a scope that was never going to work.

What if we're not sure Japan is even the right market?

That uncertainty is itself a valid starting question: it's exactly what the Validate stage exists to test, before anyone commits budget to entering.
