1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognize your device and help us understand how the site is used. This policy also covers similar technologies such as pixels, tags, and scripts, and should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

3. Your choices

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies via the cookie banner when you first visit
  • Change your preferences at any time from the cookie settings link

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the site (such as the contact form) from working.

4. Legal basis

For visitors in the EEA and UK, non-essential cookies are set on the basis of consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). Strictly necessary cookies operate on the basis of legitimate interests in running a functioning website.
